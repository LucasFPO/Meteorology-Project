Flood

What is it ?

Flood are a portion of area covered by water due to heavy rainfall.

The water can spread quickly, inundating everything ahead. Usually the

problem emerge when rivers and sewers no long are able to hold so much

water and it starts to overflow.

How is it formed ?

Warmer temperatures and increase moisture in the atmosphere are related

to heavy rainfall. Warm air can hold more moisture than cool air, so when

the warmer air is cooled and the moisture condenses, it often rains more heavily.

Consequences

Heavy rainfall in a short period of time can cause innumerous damage

as it inundate everything. Cars, house, buildings and electric power

can suffer, causing hundreds of thousands of money in loss. When massive,

flood are capable to put an entire city underwater. And even when no longer

is raining, the water can take a long time to back out.
